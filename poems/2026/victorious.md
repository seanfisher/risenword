Here was truth foretold: 
I will come, victory win
Glory to Father

Faith, hope, trust we choose
Earth’s joy full, awaits her King
Time is near at hand

Born, grow, learn and lead
His life is perfectly lived
Dreaded walk ahead

Bitter cup to drink
Heels to pierce, a head to crush
As He bled He did

Suffering spring day
Two mournful nights, third day new
For us chains broken

A promise is kept
He is risen as He said
Victory is won
