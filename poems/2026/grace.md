Take His hand, He’ll be your sight,
Guide you through your darkest night,
For what now seems to be a wall,
Standing impenetrable, tall, 
Cannot hide you from His light.

He feels the pain you’re going through
But He is standing right by you 
Let His presence quell your fear 
Feel His Spirit drawing near 
Rest in His love, His love so dear 

Don’t look behind , don’t look ahead 
Just fix your eyes on Him instead 
Learn to trust, don’t let Him go, 
For you must always, always know,
Just how much He loves you so. 

He came to teach, He came to give 
To show you how to really live 
The Father sent His  only Son, 
For Father, Son are truly One, 
Their constant love will overcome. 

You are entrusted with His grace 
So never turn away your face 
He is the shepherd who will save 
The lost and broken when they stray 
Stay close to Him and walk His way.

He brings hope to the oppressed 
He whispers peace to the depressed 
To the tired and overburden, He gives rest
To those that fall behind he says 
“Come walk with Me to better days”

He shed tears In Gethsemane
Sweated blood in mental agony
Yet accepted, with humility,
The suffering that lay ahead
The crown of thorns upon his head
The cross to which he would be led. 

Rejection at the hands of those
Some who followed, some He chose, 
Seeking shadows as the cockerel crows.
Those Whom he'd come, in love. to save
Once waving palms, now fists of rage
Sought to consign Him to a grave. 

Christ made the greatest sacrifice 
Upon a cross, he lost his life 
They took his broken body down
Then laid Him in a burial gown 
Yet In three days another crown 
Rested on the risen Saviour’s brow. 

He waits for you, He longs to see ,
The chains of sin and guilt fall free 
The Father, Son and Spirit three
The Blessed Holy Trinity
Will free you from your earthly ties 
Let your soul, your spirit, rise
Live with Him in Paradise........
