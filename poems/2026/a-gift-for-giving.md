A loaf of bread, a cookie batch,
Candy or a sandwich - wrap

A kindly word, a loving smile
A gift of sharing for awhile.

And so this season, full of joy,
With these continue through the year.

Some pretty wraps for bread or batch
To bless a friend’s or neighbor’s latch.
