When he climbed the hill to Calvary
the pain he felt was misery
he carried his cross up the hill
when he was nailed down
but what did he say
Lord forgive the people for the sins they made
and then his friends took him away
away in a tomb guarded by soldiers
three days will pass and then Mary will see
that he is resurrected you see
and that is my story and the meaning of Easter
