A - As he said, he went on the cross
S - Souls were hopeless, all was lost
H - His carriers sobbed on the death
E - Elizabeth and Mary came to the tomb
S - Sadly Mary said, "this is our doom"
A - Angels heard her and she felt better
I - "Is he here?" she said, and the Angel said, "He is Risen"
D - Done she was, but before she left she heard a footstep and a voice...
.  - ..."Mary," he said, "I have risen as I said."
