Gentle breezes coming down 
There you see a baby born
In Bethlehem 
Prince of Peace
King of Kings
God of Gods
Peace, Peace, Peace 
You might give gifts
They won't accept
Myrrh, frankincense, gold
Jewels worth diamonds 
Gently kneeling down to Him
The new baby King is born
